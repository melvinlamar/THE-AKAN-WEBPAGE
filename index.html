Every culture is unique in some way, the Akan tribe in Ghana, La Cote d’Voire and part of Southern America have every rich culture concerning the names given to their children.


Akan (Twi, Asante) names are the names used by the Akan people of Ghana and the Ivory Coast, who are frequently naming their baby boys and baby girls after the day of the week in which they were born and the order of birth for each of them, like first born Píèsíe, second born for baby boy Mǎnu/ for baby girl Máanu etc.
